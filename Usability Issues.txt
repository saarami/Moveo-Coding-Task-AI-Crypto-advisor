Usability Issues
1. Form layout and field order appear confusing

The placement and organization of the fields do not follow a clear or commonly expected flow (e.g., full name → billing address → payment details).

Potential impact:
May confuse users and make the form harder to complete efficiently.

2. “MI” abbreviation is unclear

The abbreviation “MI” may not be easily understood by all users.

Potential impact:
Users may be unsure what information is required in this field.


3. Required field indicator placement may be confusing

The red asterisk appears near the input fields rather than next to the field labels.

Potential impact:
Users may not immediately understand which labels represent required fields.

4. Payment amount does not display currency

The payment amount is shown as “30.00” without indicating the currency type.

Potential impact:
Users may not know whether the amount is in USD, EUR, or another currency.
